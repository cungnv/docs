### Key Features of Our Advanced Endpoints

#### Employee Search (Asynchronous)

1. **Initiate Search**: Submit criteria to the `/search-employees` endpoint to start. 
2. **Monitor Progress**: Use the `/check-search-status` endpoint to track your search.
3. **Retrieve Results**: Access results via `/get-search-results` with minimal cost per profile.

#### Company Search (Asynchronous)

1. **Initiate Search**: Use `/search-companies` to start your company search.
2. **Monitor Progress**: Track the status of your search at no additional cost.
3. **Retrieve Results**: Fetch company data with low credit costs per profile.
