title: "Quickstart"
description: "Our API is operated through [Rapid API marketplace](https://rapidapi.com/). It's so simple and straight forward try it out."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register an account with Rapid">
    It takes less than a minute to sign up [here](https://rapidapi.com/auth/sign-up).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the right plan for your business">
    Once signed in to Rapid, please select the [plan](https://rapidapi.com/freshdata-freshdata-default/api/fresh-linkedin-profile-data) that fits your demand. The BASIC plan is free forever and does not require a credit card. However, to use the advanced LinkedIn search feature (via sales navigator), you'd need at least a PRO plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it out">
    Once you subscribed to one of our plans, please to go the playground and test the endpoints. It does include sample code snippets in popular languages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
