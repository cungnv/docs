---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register an Account with Rapid">
    Signing up takes less than a minute. Get started [here](https://rapidapi.com/auth/sign-up){:target="_blank"}.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the Right Plan for Your Business">
    After signing in to Rapid, select the [plan](https://rapidapi.com/freshdata-freshdata-default/api/fresh-linkedin-profile-data) that meets your needs. The BASIC plan is free forever and requires no credit card. For advanced LinkedIn search capabilities (via Sales Navigator), consider upgrading to at least the PRO plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test It Out">
    Once subscribed to a plan, head over to the playground to test the endpoints. The playground includes sample code snippets in popular programming languages, making it easy to integrate into your projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>
